📋 How to Get Your M-Pesa API Credentials

  1. Go to developer.safaricom.co.ke
  2. Create an account or login
  3. Go to "My Apps" and click "Create App"
  4. Select "Lipa Na M-Pesa Sandbox" as the product
  5. Copy your Consumer Key and Consumer Secret
  6. For Paybill, get your Passkey from the same portal
  7. Enter all details above and click "Test Connection"
